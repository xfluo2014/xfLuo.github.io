Research Interests

My research focuses on blockchain foundational principles and application technologies. Key areas include:

  • Lightweight Blockchain Systems
  • Consensus Protocols
  • Scheduling Algorithms
  • Off-chain Blockchain Networks
Lightweight Consensus: Optimizing blockchain consensus algorithms to reduce computational and communication overhead, making the system suitable for resource-constrained environments such as IoT devices.
Off-chain Networks: Utilizing off-chain solutions to scale blockchain systems, enabling faster transaction processing while reducing on-chain load by performing operations outside the main blockchain and only submitting final results.
